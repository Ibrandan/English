Karen and the Artist

Main Characters: Karen and Antonio

Characters

  • Karen's Father
  • Bartman
  • The woman at the art gallery.
  • The man at the art gallery
  • Giorgio

Karen works as a flight attendant, she travels constantly. When she comes back to her father’s house in London, they talk about art. After day she flys to Roma, When she was in a café, she sees a lot of pictures on the wall. She discover that the artist is poor and gives his pictures as payment for the food. She meets the artist at Leonardo's cafe, Antonio Brunetti. He wants to paint Karen. Karen decides to help him out by selling his pictures in art galleries.

Karen shows her father Antonio’s pictures, and thinks it will be difficult to sell because the artist is unknown. Karen’s first attempt was unsuccessful, but she learns how to sell and sell Antonio’s pictures in London, Paris and Amsterdam. When she goes back to Rome, Antonio is ill. Karen tells him that she has sold his pictures. The Antonio’s friend, Giorgio, suggests that they work together: Antonio paints, and Karen sells. Karen agrees to do so.
